Narrator: Just after drinking the sharbat, Lambah fell on the ground.

Narrator: A strange change began in his body.

3. सूत्रधारः - तस्य पतनस्य ध्वनिम् श्रुत्वा जॉन-वैज्ञानिकः आयस-सिंहः च तम् प्रति धावितौ।
Narrator: Hearing the sound of his fall, Scientist John and Ayasa Singh ran towards him.
