2.10.1

यदा [पापया] कुब्जया मन्थरया देवी कैकेयी भृशम् दुर्बोधिता । When the queen
Kaikeyi was exceedingly misguided by the sinful hunchback Manthara.

तदा [विषाक्त-बाण-विद्धया] किन्नर्या इव तया कैकेय्या भूमौ शयितम् । Then, she,
Kaikeyi, lay down on the ground like a Kinnari struck by a poisoned arrow.

2.10.2

[भामिन्या] कैकेय्या कृत्यम् "[सम्यक्]" इति मनसा निश्चितम् । The beautiful
Kaikeyi decided the strategy in her mind as, "Excellent!"

[विचक्षणा] कैकेयी मन्थरायै शनैः सर्वम् अवोचत् । The intelligent Kaikeyi slowly
told everything to Manthara.

2.10.3

[मन्थरा-वाक्य-मोहितया] [दीनया] कैकेय्या निश्चयः कृतः । Deluded by Manthara's
words, the miserable Kaikeyi made a decision.

[भामिन्या] कैकेय्या नाग-कन्यया इव दीर्घम् उष्णम् च निश्वसितम् । The passionate
Kaikeyi sighed deeply and hotly like a female serpent.

2.10.4

तया कैकेय्या मुहूर्तम् [आत्म-सुख-आवहः] मार्गः चिन्तितः । For a moment, that path
bringing happiness to herself was thought about by her, Kaikeyi.

तया [सुहृदा] [अर्थ-कामया] च मन्थरया सः सुनिश्चयः निशमितः । That firm resolve was
ascertained by her, the friendly and well-wishing Manthara.

2.10.5

मन्थरा [परम-प्रीता] सिद्धिम् [प्राप्ता] इव भूता । Manthara became extremely
pleased, as if she had attained success.

अथ [रुषितया] देव्या कैकेय्या [सम्यक्] विनिश्चयः कृतः । Then, a firm decision was
correctly made by the angry queen Kaikeyi.

2.10.6

[अबलया] कैकेय्या मुखे भृकुटीम् निवेश्य भूमौ शयनम् कृतम् । The weak Kaikeyi,
having placed a frown on her face, lay down on the ground.

ततः [चित्राणि] माल्यानि [दिव्यानि] आभरणानि च (कैकेय्या त्यक्तानि) । Then, the
beautiful garlands and divine ornaments were cast away (by Kaikeyi).

2.10.7

कैकेय्या [त्यक्तानि] माल्यानि आभरणानि च भूमिम् पतितानि । The garlands and
ornaments, cast away by Kaikeyi, fell to the ground.

तया कैकेय्या तानि माल्यानि आभरणानि च [त्यक्तानि] । Those garlands and ornaments
were cast away by her, Kaikeyi.

2.10.8

यथा नक्षत्रैः नभः शोभितम् (तथा) (तैः माल्याभरणैः) वसुधा शोभिता । As the sky was
adorned by stars, (so) the earth was adorned (by those garlands and ornaments).

[मलिन-अम्बरा] [निपतिता] सा कैकेयी क्रोधागारे दृष्टा । Wearing soiled garments,

2.10.9

Having firmly bound her hair into a single braid, Kaikeyi lay on the ground like
a lifeless Kinnari.

महाराजेन दशरथेन च राघवस्य अभिषेचनम् आज्ञापितम् । And the installation ceremony
of Raghava (Rama) was ordered by the great king Dasharatha.

2.10.10

coronation is indeed certain," thus was known by the great king Dasharatha.

सा कैकेयी भूयः भर्तारम् परिपीडयितुम् उपक्रान्ता । She, Kaikeyi, once more
proceeded to torment her husband.
