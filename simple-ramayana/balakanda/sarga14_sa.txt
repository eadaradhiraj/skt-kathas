1.14.1
अथ [पूर्णे] संवत्सरे, तस्मिन् तुरङ्गमे प्राप्ते, राज्ञः दशरथस्य यज्ञः सरय्वाः उत्तरे तीरे आरब्धः अभूत्।
Then, in the [completed] year, when that horse had returned, the sacrifice of King Dasharatha did begin on the northern bank of the Sarayu.

1.14.2
[द्विजर्षभाः] [सुमहात्मनः] अस्य राज्ञः दशरथस्य [अश्वमेधे] महायज्ञे, ऋष्यशृङ्गं पुरस्कृत्य, कर्म कृतवन्तः अभूवन्।
The [bulls among Brahmins], having placed Rishyashringa at the forefront, did perform the rites in the great Ashvamedha sacrifice of this [very great-souled] king, Dasharatha.

1.14.3
[वेदपारगाः] याजकाः विधिवत् कर्म कृतवन्तः अभूवन्। ते शास्त्रतः, यथाविधि, यथान्यायं च परिक्रमितवन्तः अभूवन्।
The priests, [masters of the Vedas], did perform the rites according to the rules. They did proceed according to scripture, according to the rules, and according to custom.

1.14.4
द्विजाः शास्त्रतः प्रवर्ग्यम्, तथा एव उपसदं च कृत्वा, [अधिकम्] सर्वं कर्म अपि शास्त्रतः विधिवत् कृतवन्तः अभूवन्।
The Brahmins, having performed the Pravargya and likewise the Upasad rites according to the scriptures, did also perform all the [additional] rites according to the scriptures and the rules.

1.14.5
ततः [हृष्टाः] [सर्वे] [मुनिपुङ्गवाः] अभिपूज्य, [प्रातः-सवन-पूर्वाणि] कर्माणि यथाविधि कृतवन्तः अभूवन्।
Then [all] the [best of sages], being [joyful] and having performed the worship, did perform the rites [beginning with the morning libation] according to the rules.
